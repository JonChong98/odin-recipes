Quick and Easy Lasagna

Description

Hearty lasagna that's quick and easy to make. Able to feed a family or one very hungry adult.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cheeses, eggs and seasonings in a bowl.
  3. Add a layer of sauce to the baking dish, followed by a layer of the cheese mix. Lastly, add a layer of lasagna noodle.
  4. Repeat until baking dish is full, or out of ingredients. Leave the top layer without lasagna noodle.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, covered with aluminium foil.
  6. Uncover and bake for another 10 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and allow to cool before serving.
