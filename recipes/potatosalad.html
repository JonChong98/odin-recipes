Classic Potato Salad

Description

A classic potato salad that's easy to make with simple ingredients. Perfect for when you have guests over.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Relish
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm.
  2. Drain, cool, peel, and chop potatoes. Set potatoes aside to cool.
  3. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil, remove from heat and let stand for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove eggs from hot water. Cool, peel, and chop into chunks.
  5. Combine potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, relish, mayonnaise, mustard and seasonings in a large bowl. Mix together until well combined.
  6. Chill potato salad in the refrigerator before serving for best results.
