Japanese Curry

Description

Ingredients:

  • Beef (or Chicken, Lamb, Shrimp), chopped - 450g
  • Medium Onions, minced - 1 3/4
  • Medium Carrot, chopped - 1/2
  • Medium Potato, chopped - 1
  • Vegetable oil - 1 Tbsp
  • Water - 540ml
  • S&B Golden Curry Sauce Mix 92g - 1 pack

Steps:

  1. Stir-fry meat and vegetables with oil in large skillet on medium heat for approx. 5min.
  2. Add water and bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until ingredients are tender, approx. 15min.
  3. Turn the heat off, break S&B Golden Curry Sauce Mix into pieces and add them to the skillet. Stir until sauce mixes are completely melted. Simmer approx. 5min, stirring constantly.
  4. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
